How it Works

Getting started with SafeSurfer is quick and straightforward.

Simply install the extension with one click, then customize your settings by adding or removing terms you want to filter out. Filtered words will have a blur effect applied that cannot be copy or pasted.

SafeSurfer works seamlessly in the background, ensuring that your child's online experience is safe and tailored to your family's standards, all without slowing down their browsing.
